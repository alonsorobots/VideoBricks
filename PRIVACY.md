# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 14, 2026

## Overview

VideoBricks is a desktop application that runs entirely on your local computer. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## Data Collection

VideoBricks does **not** collect any data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry is sent
- No files or video content leave your device
- No network connections are made (except to check for updates via GitHub Releases, if applicable)
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used

## Local Data

All video processing (trimming, GIF conversion, MP4 export) happens locally on your machine. Your video files and exported outputs remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

## Third-Party Services

VideoBricks does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## AI Shot Detection

The TransNetV2 AI model used for shot boundary detection runs entirely on your local machine using ONNX Runtime. No video data is sent to any external service for processing.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted to this repository.
